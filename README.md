## 🎮 EXECUTIVE OVERVIEW

**THE VEIL: Edinburgh Echoes** is a narrative-driven supernatural adventure that blurs the boundary between reality and the spirit realm. Players assume the role of **Cassidy**, a war-haunted sound engineer, as they investigate their missing brother Jacob through a mysterious "Dead Air" frequency signal in the fog-laden streets of 1924 Edinburgh.

Combining **audio-centric puzzle mechanics** with **atmospheric storytelling**, THE VEIL transforms sound itself into a gateway between worlds—where silence becomes threat, and frequency becomes salvation.

### 🎯 Core Vision
The Veil represents a bold departure from conventional horror gaming, prioritizing **atmospheric immersion**, **audio innovation**, and **emotional resonance** over jump-scares and combat mechanics. By grounding supernatural phenomena in sound design and historical authenticity, we create a uniquely haunting experience.

## 📖 THE STORY: "The Silent Symphony"

### Setting: 1924 Edinburgh
Set in post-WWI Edinburgh's atmospheric streets, the game leverages the rich history and gothic architecture to craft an immersive experience. Players navigate both the modern Living World and the ethereal Veil World—two interconnected dimensions existing in the same physical space.

### The Hook
You are **Cassidy**, a former Sound Engineer for the British Army who specialized in acoustic location. You've come to Edinburgh because of a frequency—a low-humming "Dead Air" signal that only your modified radio equipment can pick up. This signal is connected to your brother **Jacob**, who vanished six months ago while investigating its source.

### The Mystery
The city is "leaking." The boundary between the living and the dead—**The Veil**—is thinning due to a secret experiment conducted in the underground vaults of the South Bridge. The frequency they discovered is now spreading through Edinburgh like a disease, thinning the barrier and unleashing creatures called **Siphoners**—entities that feed on life force itself.

### Your Quest
You must navigate both worlds, solving acoustic puzzles and photographing the **Memory Echoes** that bind spirits to locations. With each Echo you resolve, the Living World heals slightly, and the Veil strengthens. But time is running out, and the Siphoners grow restless.

## 🎮 CORE GAMEPLAY: Identify → Shift → Resolve

THE VEIL's innovative gameplay centers on three interconnected mechanics forming the core loop that defines all player interaction.

### Phase 1: IDENTIFY 🔍
**Objective:** Detect supernatural disturbances in the Living World

**Mechanics:**
- **Jacob Orb:** Your brother manifests as a glowing orb—your supernatural "Geiger Counter"
  - Blue glow = Safe
  - Orange glow = Cold Spot nearby
  - Red glow = Siphoner approaching
  - Intense vibration = Immediate danger

- **Modified Radio Equipment:** Detects and displays frequency information
  - Detection range: 100+ meters
  - Frequency display: 1 Hz - 20,000 Hz
  - Audio feedback helps triangulate sources
  - Limited battery: 2 hours per charge

- **Audio Cues:** Listen carefully
  - Low-frequency hum indicates proximity
  - Environmental anomalies guide investigation
  - Subtle whispers mark supernatural presences

### Phase 2: SHIFT 🌀
**Objective:** Cross the Veil into the Spirit World

**Mechanics:**
- **World Transformation:** Press 'V' to shift between dimensions
  - Visual: Buildings become translucent, colors shift to blue/silver spectrum
  - Audio: Sound muffles, ethereal otherworldly ambience emerges
  - Duration: 90 seconds maximum (sanity constraint)
  - Cooldown: 15 seconds between shifts

- **Environmental Revelation:** Hidden paths, objects, and entities become visible
  - Walls phase partially transparent
  - Spirit Tether lines glow blue
  - Lost souls become visible as translucent figures
  - Architecture transforms, sometimes expanding to impossible scales

- **Sanity System:** Prolonged Veil exposure drains mental fortitude
  - Drain rate: 1 sanity per 2 seconds
  - Visual distortion at critical levels
  - Audio hallucinations worsen
  - Forced ejection if depleted completely
  - Recovery: Return to Living World (+2 sanity/second)

### Phase 3: RESOLVE ✨
**Objective:** Permanently heal supernatural disturbances

**Sub-Mechanics:**

#### Echo Photography 📸
- Photograph the "object out of place" tethering a Memory Echo
- Identify anachronistic item among period-correct environment
- Difficulty scales: Obvious objects (unlimited time) → Complex sequences (90 seconds)

#### Frequency Tuning 🎵
- Align audio slider to match ghost's "hum" signature
- Listen carefully and adjust slider to match frequency
- Difficulty scales: Single frequency (±10% tolerance) → Harmonic chords (±2% tolerance)
- Time limits: 30-90 seconds depending on difficulty

#### Strategic Confrontation ⚡
- **Camera Flash:** Your only offensive tool
  - Stuns Siphoners for 3 seconds
  - Range: 10 meters
  - Cooldown: 5 seconds between flashes
  - Limited battery: 5 flashes per location
  - Creates escape window for evasion

## 🌍 GAME MECHANICS IN DEPTH

### Audio-Centric Gameplay Philosophy
Sound is **PRIMARY GAMEPLAY MEDIUM**, not supplementary:
- Frequency Detection System
- Audio Pattern Matching Puzzles
- Siphoner Detection via Sound
- Jacob Orb Audio Cues
- Environmental Soundscapes as Gameplay Element

### The Siphoners: Adaptive Threats
**Nature:** Entities born from the failed experiment at the boundary between life and death

**Behavior States:**
1. **Passive:** Idle patrol, minimal threat
2. **Alert:** Detected player audio, heightened awareness
3. **Hunting:** Active pursuit, relentless aggression
4. **Feeding:** Contact achieved, instant death consequence

**Detection Rules:**
- **Hearing:** Detect footsteps from 20 meters away
- **Frequency:** Attracted to radio broadcasts within 100 meters
- **Proximity:** Automatic detection within 10 meters
- **Blindness:** Cannot see in Living World (audio hunters only)

### World State System
**Dynamic Environment:**
- Track location-by-location healing percentage (0-100%)
- Environmental changes reflect progress:
  - 0-25%: Heavy fog, oppressive atmosphere, multiple Siphoners
  - 25-50%: Fog clearing, colors returning, fewer threats
  - 50-75%: Clear skies, vibrant lighting, peaceful zones emerge
  - 75-100%: Full restoration, no threats, natural beauty

## 👥 CHARACTER ECOSYSTEM

### Cassidy (Protagonist)
**Age:** 32 | **Background:** Former British Army Sound Engineer | **Trauma:** Loss of brother Jacob, PTSD from WWI

**Unique Ability:** Exceptional auditory processing—can identify frequencies by ear, understand audio patterns intuitively

**Character Arc:** From skepticism to acceptance; from isolation to connection; from grief to purpose

### Jacob (The Orb)
**Status:** Caught between worlds, partially consumed by Siphoner during failed experiment

**Function:** Navigation compass through Cold Spot detection, emotional anchor for Cassidy's motivation, repository of hidden knowledge about the experiment

**Mystery:** Can Jacob be fully freed, or will he remain eternally tethered?

### The Siphoners: Malevolent Void Entities

**Tier 1: Feral Siphoners**
- Humanoid voids, 2 meters tall
- Animal-level intelligence, mindless hunger
- Threat Level: High (one-hit kill)

**Tier 2: Corrupted Siphoners**
- Partially formed, bearing human traces
- Human-equivalent intelligence, strategic hunting
- Threat Level: Very High

**Tier 3: The Primordial**
- Unique, ancient entity
- Superhuman intelligence, communicative
- Threat Level: Extreme (final confrontation)

### Memory Echoes: Spectral Snapshots

**Type 1: Spectators**
- Neutral entities replaying traumatic moments
- Non-threatening, seek resolution

**Type 2: Conflicted**
- Torn between acceptance and rage
- React to player presence, may assist or hinder

**Type 3: Hostile**
- Corrupted by Siphoner influence
- Attack during resolution attempts

**Type 4: The Bound**
- Partially consumed by Siphoners
- Carry memories of void contact

## 🗺️ PRIMARY LOCATION: Mary King's Close

### Historical Significance
Mary King's Close is a real 17th-century alleyway buried beneath Edinburgh's Royal Mile, sealed after the Great Plague of 1645. For centuries it remained hidden—a perfect setting for supernatural narrative.

### Level Structure: 6 Zones

| Zone | Duration | Difficulty | Purpose |
|------|----------|-----------|---------|
| **Entry Chamber** | 5-8 min | Beginner | Tutorial introduction |
| **East Corridor** | 10-15 min | Beginner → Standard | Expanded exploration |
| **Main Chamber** | 15-20 min | Standard | Central hub |
| **West Corridor** | 12-18 min | Standard → Advanced | Advanced puzzles |
| **Central Vault** | 20-25 min | Advanced → Hardcore | Climactic confrontation |
| **Secret Chamber** | 10-15 min | Extreme | Hidden lore (post-game) |

### Progression & Difficulty Curve

**Zones 1-2 (0-25% Playtime):**
- Safe exploration, learning mechanics
- Minimal Siphoner encounters
- Encouraging feedback

**Zones 2-3 (25-50% Playtime):**
- Moderate difficulty increase
- First real Siphoner threat
- Heightening tension

**Zones 3-4 (50-75% Playtime):**
- Significant challenge spike
- Complex multi-stage puzzles
- Constant Siphoner presence

**Zones 4-5 (75-100% Playtime):**
- Maximum difficulty
- High-stakes decisions
- Emotional climax

## 🔊 AUDIO DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

THE VEIL's success depends on revolutionary audio design. Sound is **PRIMARY GAMEPLAY MECHANIC**, not atmosphere.

### Core Audio Principles
- **Diegetic Priority:** Footsteps, breathing, environmental sounds are mechanically important
- **Frequency Landscape:** Each entity, location, emotion has unique frequency signature
- **Spatial Audio:** 3D sound positioning essential for navigation
- **Real-Time Synthesis:** Frequencies generate dynamically based on puzzle state

### Frequency Architecture

| Frequency Range | Association | Use |
|---|---|---|
| 1-2 kHz | Deep, foundational | Base experiment frequency |
| 2-4 kHz | Emotional, stirring | Memory Echo signatures |
| 4-6 kHz | Neutral, analytical | Architectural resonance |
| 6-8 kHz | Ethereal, transcendent | Veil boundaries |
| 8-10 kHz | Harsh, dangerous | Siphoner warnings |
