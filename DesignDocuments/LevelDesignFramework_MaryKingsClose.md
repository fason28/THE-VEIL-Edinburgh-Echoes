# Level Design Framework for Mary King's Close

## Overview
This document outlines a comprehensive Level Design Framework for Mary King’s Close, organized into six distinct zones. Each zone will detail layouts, measurements, puzzles, audio landscape, and progression mechanics.

---

## Zones Overview
1. **Entry Chamber**
2. **East Corridor** (7 alcoves)
3. **Main Chamber**
4. **West Corridor** (4 landings)
5. **Central Vault**
6. **Secret Chamber**

---

### 1. Entry Chamber
- **Dimensions**: 10m x 6m
- **Atmospheric Details**: Dim lighting, cobwebs, echo of distant water drips.
- **Puzzles**: Unlock an ancient door by solving a riddle inscribed on the wall.
- **Audio Landscape**: Soft whispers, footsteps, and creaking wood.

---

### 2. East Corridor
- **Dimensions**: 30m x 4m
- **Layout**: Four distinct sections with alcoves on both sides.
- **Alcove 1**: Contains a hidden lever.
- **Alcove 2**: Displays historical artifacts.
- **Alcove 3**: A mirror puzzle reflecting light to reveal a path.
- **Alcove 4**: Contains a Siphoner encounter.
- **Alcove 5**: Opens a passageway to the Main Chamber.
- **Alcove 6**: Represents a memory trigger puzzle.
- **Alcove 7**: Hides a collectible item.
- **Puzzle Frequency**: 3 encounters every playthrough.

---

### 3. Main Chamber
- **Dimensions**: 15m x 10m
- **Atmospheric Details**: Faint sounds of distant conversations; flickering lights.
- **Puzzles**: A complex statue-activation puzzle requiring players to arrange artifacts correctly to unlock the path to the West Corridor.
- **Audio Landscape**: Murmurs, ambient city noises, heavy footsteps.

---

### 4. West Corridor
- **Dimensions**: 30m x 6m
- **Layout**: Features four landing platforms overlooking a lower area.
- **Landings**:
  1. **Landing 1**: Contains a missing tile puzzle.
  2. **Landing 2**: Offers a view of lore-related murals.
  3. **Landing 3**: Hides an enemy ambush.
  4. **Landing 4**: Access to the Central Vault.
- **Puzzle Frequency**: 2 encounters every playthrough.

---

### 5. Central Vault
- **Dimensions**: 12m x 12m
- **Atmospheric Details**: A sacred, quiet area; feeling of pressure in the air.
- **Puzzles**: A series of locks that requires players to collect matching symbols from previous zones.
- **Audio Landscape**: Echo of breathing and subtle shimmering sound effects.

---

### 6. Secret Chamber
- **Dimensions**: 8m x 8m
- **Atmospheric Details**: Darker ambiance filled with artifacts; tense atmosphere.
- **Puzzles**: A timing-based challenge involving the manipulation of light sources.
- **Progression Mechanics**: Completion unlocks final lore details.
- **Siphoner Encounters**: Stronger version of previous encounters; multiple waves of enemies.
- **World State Changes**: Completing this chamber ensures player safety from Siphoners.
- **Audio Landscape**: Heartbeat sounds, whispers, and overwhelming silence.

---
