# LEVEL DESIGN FRAMEWORK: Mary King's Close
## THE VEIL: Edinburgh Echoes

---

## 1. LOCATION OVERVIEW

### Historical Context
- **Real Location:** Mary King's Close, Royal Mile, Edinburgh
- **Historical Period:** 1680s - Present
- **History:** Sealed underground street closed off following the Great Plague of 1645
- **Modern Status:** Tourist attraction with restricted access
- **Relevance to Story:** Site of the secret South Bridge experiment that weakened the Veil

### Level Purpose
Mary King's Close serves as THE VEIL's primary playable area, introducing all core mechanics while establishing atmospheric foundation for subsequent locations. The level transitions from safe exploration to escalating supernatural tension.

---

## 2. WORLD DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

### Living World vs. Veil World

#### Living World (1924)
- **Time Period:** Modern era (1920s)
- **Architecture:** Preserved historic with modern restoration
- **Lighting:** Electric lights, gas lamps, daylight filtering through grates
- **Atmosphere:** Slightly eerie tourist location; narrow passages, stone walls
- **Audio:** Footsteps echo, occasional distant voices, creaking doors
- **Accessibility:** Open to player exploration with some locked passages
- **Danger Level:** Low (no immediate threats)
- **Sanity Impact:** Neutral

#### Veil World
- **Time Period:** 1680s - multitemporal layers
- **Architecture:** Original medieval construction, partially ruined
- **Lighting:** Blue ethereal glow, natural bioluminescence from Tether lines
- **Atmosphere:** Haunting, shifting, labyrinthine, walls breathe
- **Audio:** Ghostly whispers, harmonic drones, distant screams (muffled)
- **Accessibility:** Expanded, revealing hidden chambers and passages
- **Danger Level:** High (multiple Siphoners, temporal hazards)
- **Sanity Impact:** -1 per 2 seconds in Veil

### Visual Contrast Example
**Location: Main Chamber**
- **Living World:** 40m x 30m stone chamber, electric lighting casts sharp shadows, tourist barriers present
- **Veil World:** Same space expands to 80m x 60m, architecture multiplies, walls become translucent, blue mist obscures edges, Tether lines crisscross the space
