# Character & Lore Bible for THE VEIL

## Cassidy
- **Age**: 32
- **Former Role**: Sound Engineer  
- **Background**: Cassidy is a seasoned sound engineer who finds solace in the world of sound but has been deeply scarred by his experiences in World War I. The war has left him with profound trauma, shaping his personality and perspective on life. He often translates his emotional turmoil into his work, creating sounds that reflect both beauty and pain.  
- **Relationships**: Cassidy's trauma often distances him from others, yet he connects deeply with those who share similar scars.

## Jacob (The Orb)
- **Description**: Jacob is a mysterious entity caught between worlds. His existence is tied to the central narrative of THE VEIL, embodying the struggles of dimensional separation and the quest for identity.
- **Role in Narrative**: Jacob serves as a guide for other characters, often providing insight into the greater struggles of existence. His perspective forces characters to confront their own realities.

## Siphoner Tiers
1. **Feral**: 
   - Description: The initial tier of Siphoners, characterized by uncontrolled hunger and primal instincts. These beings act on impulse without coherent thought.  
   - Impact on Narrative: Represents the chaos and loss of self that characters fear.

2. **Corrupted**: 
   - Description: These Siphoners retain some memories of their former selves but are deeply influenced by their corrupted nature. Conflict within them leads to unpredictable behavior.  
   - Impact on Narrative: They symbolize the struggle between the past and the present, often reminding other characters of what they've lost.

3. **Primordial**: 
   - Description: The most powerful Siphoners, they possess ancient wisdom and great strength. They exist nearly outside of normal time and space, representing the culmination of Siphoner transformation.  
   - Impact on Narrative: Serve as the ultimate challenge for characters, representing their innermost fears and desires.

## Memory Echo Types
1. **Spectators**:  
   - Description: These Echoes observe but do not interfere. They are remnants of the past, showing what once was.  
   - Relationship to Narrative: They provide characters with insight and clarity about their history.

2. **Conflicted**:  
   - Description: Echoes that struggle between two memories or emotions, often manifesting as apparitions.  
   - Relationship to Narrative: Serve as a mirror for characters' own inner conflicts.

3. **Hostile**:  
   - Description: These Echoes are a projection of unresolved traumas; they act aggressively, feeding on fear and doubt.  
   - Relationship to Narrative: Challenge characters to confront their fears head-on.

4. **The Bound**:  
   - Description: Echoes that are tethered to a specific event or individual. Their purpose is linked to unfinished business.  
   - Relationship to Narrative: These Echoes often guide characters towards resolution or redemption.

## Relationships in THE VEIL
The interactions between Cassidy, Jacob, and the different Siphoner and Memory Echo types create a web of connections that drives the narrative forward. Each character's struggle reflects the themes of trauma, loss, and the search for identity embedded in THE VEIL. Characters' interactions with Siphoners and Echoes challenge their perceptions and drive personal growth throughout the story.
